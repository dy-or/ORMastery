Why OR Is AI-Resistant
🧠

AI is a tool, not the decision-maker

Machine learning predicts. Operations Research decides. Companies still need humans who can frame constraints, define objectives, and validate solutions in real-world business contexts.

⚖️

Optimization is the engine inside AI

Every LLM, every recommendation engine, every logistics AI runs on optimization algorithms. OR professionals build and maintain the very foundation AI stands on.

🏭

Real-world constraints can't be automated away

Supply chain disruptions, regulatory limits, union rules, budget caps — these require expert judgment to model correctly. No AI can replace that context.
